Wave Pool


Wave Pool is an interactive learning experience that uses gestures as a tool to understand the physics of sound.



By:  Katherine Bennett, Ibrahim Ekin, Gabe Liberti, Dave Rife, and Kevin Siwoff




How might we  engage  students using  embodied learning  to  inspire  creative inquiry in the classroom?




Our focus is on 
 middle school - high school 
 students

Our tools:


    • XBox Kinect
    • Hand/Skeleton tracking software (OpenNI library for C++)
    • Max/MSP
    • HTML5/Javascript
